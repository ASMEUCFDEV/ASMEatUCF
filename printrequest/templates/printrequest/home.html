3D PRINTER
​
We have a DeltaMaker , which uses PLA plastic. All money collected will go towards buying more filament and upgrades for the printer. If you want to print something, fill out and submit the form below.

Some examples of our prints:
  • T-Rex Skull $18
  • Elephant $4
  • Octopus Phone Stand $3
Pricing is based on print time and printing material: $0.10 per gram of material + $1.00 per hour of print time.
(Price for non members is $0.20 per gram of material + $1.00 per hour of print time.)
We keep white and black filament in stock. For other colors please ask. If there is enough interest, we will purchase a new color. We also offer a glow-in-the-dark filament that costs $0.20 per gram.

For self-designed models, we encourage you to come to our office (EGN 1 Room 140) to consult our printing team to optimize your part for printing. Although 3D printing allows for advanced manufacturing, it does have its limitations.

Model your own with Solidworks (Instructions) or Autodesk Inventor. Both are free for students
